Trading Education & Strategies

Build your knowledge foundation

Technical Analysis

Learn to read charts, identify patterns, and use indicators to predict market movements. Technical analysis helps you understand price action and market trends.

  • Chart patterns and candlestick analysis
  • Technical indicators (RSI, MACD, Moving Averages)
  • Support and resistance levels
  • Volume analysis and market depth

Fundamental Analysis

Understand how economic factors, company performance, and global events influence market prices. Make informed decisions based on real market data.

  • Economic indicators and news events
  • Company financial statements
  • Industry analysis and trends
  • Geopolitical factors affecting markets

Risk Management

Protect your capital with proven risk management strategies. Learn position sizing, stop-loss orders, and portfolio diversification techniques.

  • Position sizing and money management
  • Risk-reward ratio optimization
  • Stop-loss and take-profit strategies
  • Portfolio diversification principles

Trading Psychology

Master your emotions and develop discipline. Trading psychology is often the difference between success and failure in the markets.

  • Emotional control and discipline
  • Overcoming fear and greed
  • Developing a trading plan
  • Maintaining consistency and patience

Types of Trading

Choose your trading style

Day Trading

Buy and sell securities within the same trading day. Requires quick decision-making and constant market monitoring. High potential returns but demands full attention.

Swing Trading

Hold positions for several days to weeks, capitalizing on expected price swings. Less time-intensive than day trading while still offering good profit potential.

Position Trading

Long-term trading strategy holding positions for months or years. Focuses on major market trends and requires patience and strong fundamental analysis skills.

Scalping

Ultra-short-term trading making dozens of trades per day. Aims for small profits per trade that add up to significant gains. Requires advanced tools and low latency.
