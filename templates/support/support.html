Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I verify my account?

To verify your account, log in, go to Settings, and complete the identity verification process. You'll need valid ID and proof of address.

What is the minimum deposit amount?

The minimum initial deposit is $100. You can deposit more at any time from your dashboard.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawals typically process within 1-3 business days depending on your bank and payment method.

What fees apply to deposits?

We don't charge deposit fees. However, your bank may charge fees depending on your payment method and location.

Can I trade on mobile?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices. You can trade anytime, anywhere.

What is the bonus for new traders?

New traders receive a welcome bonus on their first deposit. Check your account for eligibility and bonus details.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your data and transactions.

How do I contact support?

Use the contact methods below or visit our Support Center for live chat, email, or phone support.
